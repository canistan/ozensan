CF-13.3 B

The flexible floor saw is particularly suitable for light road rehabilitation as well as for gardening and landscaping. The cutting depth is adjusted by handwheel, and excavation is carried out via a low-maintenance rocker. The operator's handle is rubber-mounted and therefore particularly low-vibration. Due to its compact dimensions and easy handling, the machine is also ideal for the rental business.

Features & Characteristics

  • Reliable Honda petrol engine with cyclone air filter
  • Quick, easy cutting side change
  • Infinitely variable cutting depth adjustment by means of a hand crank
  • Adjustable cutting depth indicator and parking brake
  • Height-adjustable and low-vibration push bar
  • Pull-out transport handle, crane eye
